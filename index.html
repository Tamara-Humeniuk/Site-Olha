Ukrainian linguist

Scientific interest

- migration processes in Donbass (Ukraine) and the impact on regional language policy, language conflicts in a bilingual environment (Book «Ukrainian language in Donetsk region», 2015);

- Ukrainian Language testing and citizenship (case of the National Commission on State Language Standards in organizing and conducting exams for Ukrainian citizenship, international cooperation and implementation of the European experience in Ukraine);

- early bilingual and multilingual development of Ukrainian children (postdoctoral research);
